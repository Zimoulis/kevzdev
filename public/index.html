A Brief Introduction

Hi, and welcome to my site. My name is Kevin and I am a software developer and I have a passion for learning and creating new things. Ever since I was young I enjoyed finding out how things work under the hood and using that knowledge to be creative and build interesting things. Ultimately, once i took my first programming course I became excited for all the potential things that I could create. Since then I have just been honing in on my skills as a developer and always trying to improve and devlop

My background has been primarily in the backend working with stacks like Java, Spring, Hibernate, and various relational databases at my job. However, outside of work I have been falling in love with some of the web development stacks such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Nodejs. It has been a blast working with them with so many neat things to learn.

Throughout this site I hope showcase some of projects and write about some new some of the things that interest me. In time I hope to have allot of projects and content that you may find interesting or useful. So welcome, and come on in see what i am all about. I hope that you will find something here that is interesting in for you.
